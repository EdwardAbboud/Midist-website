"Midist is a Lebanese producer intrigued by sound of all waveforms and frequencies. As a former drummer in psychedelic rock and metal in Beirut, Midist uses raw sounds to create a comforting journey.

Expect atmospheric sound scapes, raw drops, groovy bass lines and rhythms from an oriental perspective. Discovering the sounds of his roots from afar allows Midist to blend the Eastern philosophies from a Western understanding.

Using experiences and memories to bring cinematic visions to life, Midist is currently active as a DJ, Producer, Musician and Sound Designer.

Follow the journey of a Middle-Eastern spirit held hostage by artistic tendencies on the fine line between the East and the West."
